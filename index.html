Listen to my heart is a project by artist, Joseph Chiocchi in which he logs records his heart beats through a logging device as well as broadcast them live.

The artist then goes back over the day's information and logs an emotional journal of the day's events relating sudden spikes as indicators of excitement or stress as well as the inverse; a calm heart beat indicating relaxation or mindfulness.

This project is currently in the funding stage.
